Our story.

By students for students

The idea for Studeroo came while I was studying for my final high school exams in Finland. I kept thinking — we have apps that track our runs, our workouts, even our sleep... so why not our studying? What if I could see other people's progress too? I knew that would motivate me to sit down and start. That's where Studeroo began: a simple, social way to log your study sessions and stay accountable — together.
